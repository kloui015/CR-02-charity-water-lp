About Us

We imagine a world where every person has access to clean and safe drinking water. Our local partners build and maintain community‑owned systems while we provide training and transparent reporting.

Why Water?

Clean water changes everything — health, education, and livelihoods. When water is close and safe, children can attend school and families can thrive.

Health

Reduces waterborne disease and improves nutrition.

Education

Kids — especially girls — spend less time collecting water.

Economy

More time for work means stronger, more resilient communities.
